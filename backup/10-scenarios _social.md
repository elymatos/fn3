# Social Domain

## Social Roles and Identities

**Domain:** Social  
**Subdomain:** Social Roles and Identities

### Scenario 1: Role Recognition and Social Positioning

**Description:** In this scenario, a #SocialActor recognizes different #SocialRoles within #SocialContexts and understands #RoleExpectations associated with various #SocialPositions. The actor identifies #StatusMarkers and #IdentityIndicators that signal role membership and adjusts #SocialBehavior accordingly to match #RoleRequirements.

**Elements:**

- **SocialActor** (Entity): Person navigating and responding to social role systems
- **SocialRoles** (Entity): Defined positions with associated behaviors and expectations
- **SocialContexts** (Entity): Situations where role-based interactions occur
- **RoleExpectations** (Entity): Behavioral standards associated with specific positions
- **SocialPositions** (State): Relative standings within social hierarchies
- **StatusMarkers** (Entity): Visible indicators of social position or role
- **IdentityIndicators** (Entity): Signs that communicate role membership or social identity
- **SocialBehavior** (Process): Actions adjusted to fit social role requirements
- **RoleRequirements** (Entity): Obligations and behaviors expected of role occupants

**Relations:**

- SocialActor recognizes SocialRoles within SocialContexts
- SocialRoles carry specific RoleExpectations and RoleRequirements
- SocialActor identifies StatusMarkers and IdentityIndicators
- StatusMarkers signal SocialPositions within hierarchies
- IdentityIndicators communicate role membership
- SocialActor adjusts SocialBehavior to match RoleExpectations
- SocialBehavior must satisfy RoleRequirements for appropriate role performance
