## Social Roles and Identities

**Domain:** Social  
**Subdomain:** Social Roles and Identities

### Scenario 1: Role Recognition and Social Positioning

**Description:** In this scenario, a #SocialActor recognizes different #SocialRoles within #SocialContexts and understands #RoleExpectations associated with various #SocialPositions. - **IdentityIndicators** (Entity): Signs that communicate role membership or social identity
- **SocialBehavior** (Process): Actions adjusted to fit social role requirements
- **RoleRequirements** (Entity): Obligations and behaviors expected of role occupants

**Relations:**

- SocialActor recognizes SocialRoles within SocialContexts
- SocialRoles carry specific RoleExpectations and RoleRequirements
- SocialActor identifies StatusMarkers and IdentityIndicators
- StatusMarkers signal SocialPositions within hierarchies
- IdentityIndicators communicate role membership
- SocialActor adjusts SocialBehavior to match RoleExpectations
- SocialBehavior must satisfy RoleRequirements for appropriate role performance
